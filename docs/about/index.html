About ResearchRadar

ResearchRadar helps researchers move from passive literature alerts to active, configurable research surveillance.

What it is

A customizable literature intelligence platform for live, scored, shareable research dashboards.

Who it is for

Scientists, labs, analysts, reviewers, journal clubs, and open-source research communities.

Open-source philosophy

The academic version prioritizes transparent scoring, reproducible configs, public templates, and self-hostable dashboards.
