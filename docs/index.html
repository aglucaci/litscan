How A Radar Gets Built

No YAML required at first. ResearchRadar generates a starter radar that can be tuned visually or exported as config.

1Enter a topic

Start with a field, disease, method, organism, or research question.

2Tune the signal

Choose sources, search terms, signal categories, and scoring weights.

3Publish the radar

Save, clone, export, and monitor a live dashboard.
